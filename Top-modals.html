Alammariyah Suite Cabin

Alammariyah edition is inspired by the amazing nature of the cliffs west of Riyadh. The cabin is covered with limestone panels to blend in the rocky mountains. The interiors brings the nature inside with the warm colors and green shade mimics the plants found in the region.

This model is 44 sqm with living area, open kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, and terrace. The living area is connected to the kitchen with full height windows to maximize the view. The external basin serves both living and bedroom, while toilet and shower are enclose with sliding door. The 3.5 by 3.5 bedroom visually connected to the terrace and the landscape with full height sliding window. In addition to the passive ventilation across the unit, all cabins are ventilated with concealed AC system.
